Pho Recipe!

Beef pho is a traditional beef soup noodle dish. It is common to find all throughout Vietnam. Here is the recipe.

Ingredients:
  • Pho noodles
  • Beef brisket, beef marrow, beef tripe
  • Pho spices
  • Onion and Ginger
  • Fish sauce, salt, sugar
  • water
Instructions:
  1. Toast spices in dry pan
  2. Char onions and ginger
  3. Boil all beef for 15 minutes to get rid of scum
  4. rinse off beef
  5. Put all ingrediants into a large pot, submerege all with water.
  6. Season with fish sauce, rock sugar, MSG, and salt
  7. Simmer for 6 hours. Season for taste
  8. Serve in bowl with side of vegetables.
